# AI Engine

## Introduction 🚀

The **AI Engine** is a complex system that combines [AI Agents ↗️](/concepts/agents/agents) services with a robust AI infrastructure powered by Large Language Models (LLMs).

![](../../../src/images/missing.jpeg)

The Engine excels at a variety of different tasks. This is accomplished thanks to its capacity to grasp user preferences and objectives via **contextual understanding**. The Engine examines trends and turns random input into meaningful insights by evaluating previous interactions. When uncertainty comes into play, the AI Engine seeks user feedback to verify its suggestions and recommendations correspond with the user's desires and objectives, establishing a collaborative and trust-based environment to ensure optimal implementation.

    <Callout type="info" emoji="ℹ️">
      The AI Engine is at the heart of the [DeltaV ↗️](/concepts/deltav/intro) chat and its functionalities.
    </Callout>

On top of the Engine's ability to understand different contexts, its **smart routing** feature also cover a fundamental role. In fact, it allows the Engine to carry out a careful assessment of available agents registered within the [Agentverse ↗️](/concepts/agent-services/agentverse-intro), considering their breadth of services and past performance data. This guarantees tasks are assigned to agents having the appropriate level of expertise and a track record of excellence. In this context, _trustworthiness_ is a key factor, with agents having a track record of ethical and dependable behavior given preference. This method not only assures tasks completion, but also creates a sense of dependability and efficiency in the cooperation.

The AI Engine actively anticipates and aligns with user needs, creating an ecosystem where technology becomes an ally in achieving objectives.
