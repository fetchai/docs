Upon successful log in, you will receive a prompt to grant the site access to your location. This allows DeltaV to enhance

    <Callout type="info" emoji="‚ÑπÔ∏è">
      To provide a better service, the system needs you to share your current location. This allows DeltaV to enhance your experience by offering **more pertinent and personalized services based on your current context**.

      For instance, if you are looking for ride-sharing or flights services, DeltaV will require your location to provide precise options and estimates based on your current whereabouts, such as the nearest vehicle or airport available.

    <Callout type="info" emoji="‚ÑπÔ∏è">

Next, you will be prompted to select a **Service Group** for the service you wish to query. You can choose the **All Service Groups** option if you are unsure about which category your desired service falls within.

Then, you need to choose an **objective** representing the desired task related to the service needed. If you do not see any objective for your request, select the **Something else** button and insert it manually within the **what are you interested about?** bar.

Once you provide this information, you will be able to proceed to the actual [DeltaV chat interface ‚ÜóÔ∏è](/guides/deltav/deltav-chat-interface). This step provides the platform with information about the specific service you are seeking for (e.g., **Travel** or **Finance** related services).

![](../../../src/images/guides/deltav/getting-started/concepts_deltav_getting-started.png)

Whenever ready, click on the **Start Chat** button to initiate the conversation with the **DeltaV AI Agent** and query for any service you require!
